2021 Annual Dues

The Annual Assessment is $350.00, which was due by February 1, 2021. It is considered late after February 28, 2021.

Please note, a fine of $20.00 per month beginning on March 1, 2021, will be assessed until paid in full. If the annual dues and fines are not paid by April 30, 2021, the HOA will file a lien on your property.
